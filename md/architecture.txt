# Contact Project Overview

Unified Business Communication Platform

The ultimate goal of Contact is to make communication and collaboration with anyone simple and unified.

As an AI-native contact platform, Contact will gradually automate most repetitive communication and business workflows:

• AI answers customer questions
• AI understands customer needs
• AI suggests solutions
• AI generates offers
• AI assists negotiations
• AI guides the payment process

Ultimately, users only need to review the final outcome: Deals.

## Authentication

- Use a single authentication page for both Login and Sign Up.
- Do not create separate Login and Sign Up pages.
- Users enter their email or continue with an identity provider.
- The system automatically determines whether to log in an existing user or create a new account.
- Authentication is provided by the centralized Starman Account system.
- All Starman products share the same Starman Account.
- Authentication requests are handled by account.starman.io
- After successful authentication, users are redirected back to the requesting product.
- Authentication UI should remain product-branded while using the shared Starman Account system.
